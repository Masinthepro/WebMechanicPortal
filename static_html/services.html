Comprehensive Auto Repair Services

At Pro Mechanics, we offer a full range of automotive repair and maintenance services to keep your vehicle running at its best. Our team of certified mechanics uses the latest tools and technologies to diagnose and fix issues quickly and accurately.

Engine Repair & Maintenance

Our certified technicians are experienced in diagnosing and repairing engine issues for all makes and models. We use state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment to identify problems accurately and efficiently.

Our Engine Services Include:
  • Complete engine diagnostics
  • Check engine light diagnosis
  • Engine performance testing
  • Engine repair and rebuilding
  • Timing belt replacement
  • Engine tune-ups
  • Cooling system service

Brake Service & Repair

Your vehicle's brakes are its most important safety feature. Our comprehensive brake service ensures your braking system is functioning properly for optimal safety and performance.

Our Brake Services Include:
  • Brake system inspection
  • Brake pad and shoe replacement
  • Brake rotor and drum resurfacing
  • Brake fluid flush
  • ABS system diagnostics and repair
  • Brake line inspection and replacement
  • Emergency brake adjustment
