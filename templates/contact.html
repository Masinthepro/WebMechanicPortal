FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our contact process and service appointments.

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our main phone number for immediate assistance.

While we do accept walk-ins for minor services like oil changes (subject to availability), we strongly recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure prompt service and minimize wait times. You can schedule an appointment by phone, through our contact form, or in person.

To help us serve you better, please include your vehicle's make, model, year, and mileage, along with a description of the issue or service needed. If you're experiencing specific problems, details about when they occur can help us diagnose the issue more efficiently.

Yes, we offer secure after-hours vehicle drop-off. There's a key drop box located near our main entrance with envelopes for your keys and contact information. Please call ahead to let us know you'll be using this service, and we'll contact you the next business day to discuss your service needs.

We're happy to provide general price ranges for common services over the phone or by email. However, for accurate estimates on complex repairs, we typically need to inspect the vehicle first. We always provide a detailed estimate for approval before beginning any work.
