Our Story

A Tradition of Excellence in Auto Repair

Founded in 1998 by Master Mechanic John Anderson, Pro Mechanics began as a small, family-owned shop dedicated to providing honest, high-quality auto repair services. With a passion for automobiles and a commitment to exceptional customer service, John quickly built a reputation for reliability and expertise in the community.

Over the past 25 years, we've grown from a two-bay garage to our current state-of-the-art facility with ten service bays, advanced diagnostic equipment, and a team of ASE-certified technicians. Throughout our growth, we've maintained our founding principles of honesty, quality workmanship, and fair pricing.

Today, Pro Mechanics is proud to be the trusted choice for auto repair and maintenance for thousands of customers in the Mechanic City area. Whether you're dealing with a complex engine issue or just need routine maintenance, our team is committed to keeping your vehicle running at its best.

John Anderson
Founder & Master Mechanic
Our Mission

Why Choose Pro Mechanics?

At Pro Mechanics, our mission is simple: to provide honest, high-quality auto repair services at fair prices. We believe in treating every customer's vehicle as if it were our own, recommending only the services you truly need and completing repairs with precision and care.

  • Honest Service

    We build trust through transparency. We'll always explain what your vehicle needs, why it needs it, and provide upfront pricing without hidden fees.

  • Quality Workmanship

    Our ASE-certified technicians have the skills, experience, and training to diagnose and fix problems correctly the first time.

  • Fair Pricing

    We provide competitive pricing without compromising on quality. No unnecessary upsells or surprise charges.

Our Journey

25 Years of Growth & Excellence

Follow our journey from a small family shop to a leading auto repair facility.

1998

Pro Mechanics founded by John Anderson as a two-bay garage focusing on general auto repair.

2003

Expanded to a five-bay facility and introduced specialized diagnostic equipment to better serve our growing customer base.

2008

Achieved ASE Blue Seal of Excellence recognition, demonstrating our commitment to employing certified technicians.

2013

Opened our current state-of-the-art facility with ten service bays and a comfortable customer lounge.

2018

Celebrated 20 years in business and introduced eco-friendly practices including recycling and energy-efficient equipment.

2023

Marking 25 years of service with the launch of our digital appointment system and expanded services for hybrid and electric vehicles.
